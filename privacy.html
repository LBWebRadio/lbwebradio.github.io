Privacy Policy

Live Beats WebRadio · Last updated: July 1, 2026

Live Beats WebRadio ("the app") is an internet radio player. This policy explains what is stored on your device, and what limited technical data may be shared with third-party services required for the app to function.

Summary

  • No account required. We do not collect or transmit directly identifiable information such as name, email, or account identifiers.
  • No analytics, ads, or crash-reporting SDKs are used in the app.
  • Your favorites, settings, and usage data are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore.
  • The app connects directly to third-party services for radio search, streaming, and optional metadata (such as album art).

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to us:

  • Favorite stations, including "Super Favorites" and custom genre tags
  • App settings (theme, language, sleep timer, interface preferences)
  • Listening history and usage statistics (stored locally)
  • Saved song titles you manually choose
  • Optional export backups (.lbrx files) stored in a location you choose

Technical Data Shared with Third Parties

To provide core functionality, the app communicates with external services. We do not intentionally send directly identifiable information such as name, email, or account identifiers. As with all internet communication, your IP address is automatically visible to receiving servers. Only the minimum technical data required for functionality is processed.

Service Purpose Data Processed
Radio-Browser API Station search and directory Search queries, filters, votes, and play counts. IP address is visible as part of standard requests.
Streaming Servers Audio playback Direct connection to selected station stream URL.
ipwho.is API Server location lookup IP address of streaming server (used for geolocation lookup).
Apple iTunes / Last.fm Album artwork lookup Artist and track name (used to fetch images).
You can disable “Show Album Art” and “Show Server Location” in settings. When disabled, the app stops contacting these optional metadata services.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process limited technical data based on legitimate interest to provide core app functionality, including streaming and station directory services requested by the user.

Security & Data Deletion

Some radio stations use unencrypted HTTP streams, which is a limitation of the station itself. Secure HTTPS connections are used when available.

All locally stored data remains on your device. It is deleted when you clear the app’s storage or uninstall the app.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country) and does not knowingly collect personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on our website. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated version.
