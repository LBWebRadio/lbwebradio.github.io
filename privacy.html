Privacy Policy

Live Beats WebRadio · Last updated: July 1, 2026

Live Beats WebRadio ("the app"), identified by the package name com.lbwebradio.radio, is an internet radio player developed by Christian Flach. This Privacy Policy explains what data is stored on your device and what limited technical data may be processed by third-party services required for the app to function.

Summary of Data Practices

  • No account required. We do not collect or transmit directly identifiable information such as name, email, or account identifiers.
  • No analytics, ads, or crash-reporting SDKs are used.
  • All user data (favorites, settings, history) is stored locally on your device using Android DataStore.
  • The app connects directly to third-party services for radio directory, streaming, and optional metadata (e.g. album art).

Information Stored on Your Device

The following data remains strictly on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Favorite stations, including “Super Favorites” and custom genre tags
  • App settings (theme, language, sleep timer, interface preferences)
  • Listening history and usage statistics (stored locally)
  • Saved song titles selected by you
  • Optional export backups (.lbrx files stored in a user-selected location)

Technical Data Shared with Third Parties

To provide core functionality, the app communicates with external services. We do not intentionally send directly identifiable information (such as name or email). As with all internet communication, your IP address is automatically visible to receiving servers as part of standard network requests. Only the minimum technical data required for functionality is processed.

Service Purpose Data Processed
Radio-Browser API Station search and directory Search queries, filters, votes, play counts. IP address visible as part of standard requests.
Streaming Servers Audio playback Direct connection to selected station stream URL.
ipwho.is API Server location lookup IP address of streaming server (used for geolocation lookup).
Apple iTunes / Last.fm Album artwork lookup Artist and track name (used to fetch images).

You may disable “Show Album Art” and “Show Server Location” in settings. When disabled, the app stops contacting these optional metadata services, and only core streaming functionality remains active.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process limited technical data under legitimate interest, strictly to deliver core functionality such as streaming and station directory services requested by the user.

Permissions

  • Internet – Stream audio, search stations, fetch metadata
  • Notifications – Playback controls in notification area
  • Wake Lock / Foreground Service – Maintain playback in background

Security & Data Deletion

Some radio stations use unencrypted HTTP streams, which is a limitation of the station provider. Secure HTTPS connections are used when available.

All locally stored data remains on your device and is deleted when you clear the app’s storage or uninstall the app. We do not store or retain any user data on external servers.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your country) and does not knowingly collect personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on our website. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated version.
