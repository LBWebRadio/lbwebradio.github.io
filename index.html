What it does

Built around the stream, not around you.

Every feature is there to make listening easier. Nothing here is collecting data or fighting for your attention.

⬇

Browse offline

A local cache of 60,000+ stations means you can search and pick something new even without a connection.

☰

Favorites, history & backup

Save the stations you return to. Sort them your way. Back up your whole library to move it between devices.

◷

Sleep timer

Set the app to stop playback after a set time. Fall asleep to the radio without it running all night.

▤

Data usage tracking

See exactly how much mobile data your listening uses, broken down by day, month, or year.

◫

Quick Settings tile

Control playback straight from your notification shade without opening the app.

✎

Add any stream URL

Not in the directory? Add a direct stream URL manually and play it like any other station.

◐

Light on your device

A lean app with no bloated background services. Just the player, running when you need it.

🔒

Stream securely

Turn on HTTPS-only mode to block unencrypted streams. Handy peace of mind on public Wi-Fi.
