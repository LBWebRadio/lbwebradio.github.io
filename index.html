Live Beats WebRadio Player is a powerful, internet radio streaming application that gives you instant access to over 54,000 radio stations from around the world. Our internet radio player combines powerful functionality with an intuitive, beautiful interface. Track your listening habits with detailed statistics, customize themes to match your desktop style, record your favorite broadcasts with the built-in stream recorder, and enjoy seamless streaming with our optimized audio engine.
