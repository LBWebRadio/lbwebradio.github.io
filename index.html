What it does

Built around the stream, not around you.

Every feature is there to make listening easier. Nothing here is collecting data or fighting for your attention.

▶

Media3 / ExoPlayer streaming

Built on Android's own media stack for reliable, low-latency playback with live ICY metadata for track and station info.

◎

Discover thousands of stations

Search and filter by country, language, tag, codec, or bitrate. Find stations worth keeping.

☰

Favorites & history

Save the stations you return to. Back them up, and revisit anything you've played recently.

◷

Sleep timer

Set the app to stop playback after a set time. Handy for falling asleep to the radio without it running all night.

▤

Data usage tracking

See how much mobile data your listening is using, so streaming on the go doesn't come as a surprise.

◫

Quick Settings tile

Control playback straight from your notification shade without opening the app.

✎

Add any stream URL

Not in the directory? Add a direct stream URL manually and play it like any other station.

◐

Light on your device

A lean Kotlin/Compose app with no bloated background services. Just the player, running when you need it.

Personalization

26 accent colors, one tap away.

Pick the accent that matches your mood or your phone's wallpaper. It's a small detail. But it's yours to set. Nothing is chosen for you.
