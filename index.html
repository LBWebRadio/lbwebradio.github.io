Built for Android

Not a port. An Android app.

Every feature below only makes sense because it's running on your phone, not a desktop.

Background playback

Music keeps streaming through a foreground service — lock the screen, switch apps, it doesn't stop.

Quick Settings tile

Play or stop your last station straight from the notification shade, without opening the app.

Android Auto

A proper media library service, so your stations show up safely on the car display.

Data Guard

Real-time byte tracking with daily, monthly, and yearly usage — set a threshold and get warned before you blow through it.

Adaptive icon

Full support for round, square, and monochrome icon shapes — it looks native on your launcher, whatever skin you're on.

Edge-to-edge

The UI draws behind the status and navigation bars for an immersive, modern layout on every screen.
